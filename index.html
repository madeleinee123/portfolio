projects.

the key 2020

throughout her senior year of college, madeleine worked on a team of her peers helping a local nonprofit upgrade their attendance web application. the goal of the project was to iterate the design and expand the scope of a participation tracking system used by the key to better assist youth and articulate/present outcomes to donors and funding agencies. for more information check out the github page for the project.

madeleine grew up in the small town of woodstock, il famous for being the filming location of groundhog's day. this instilled an ironic love of bill murray in her at a young age. utilizing css/html and javascript, madeleine developed a game hosted by github pages built from the ground up. for more information on the design flow check out the github page for the project or better yet, play the game.
